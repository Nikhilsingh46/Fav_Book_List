My Favorite Books

Reading has always been a powerful source of learning and inspiration for me. Books not only improve my knowledge but also help me develop a better mindset and perspective towards life and success.

    

Book List

  1. Rich Dad Poor Dad: Robert Kiyosaki

    This book completely changed my understanding of money, assets and financial independence. It taught me the importance of financial education.

  2. Atomic Habits: James Clear

    A very practical book that explains how small daily habits can lead to big changes. It helped me focus on consistency and self-discipline.

  3. The Alchemist: Paulo Coelho

    An inspiring story about following your dreams and trusting the journey of life. It motivated me to believe in my goals.

  4. Think and Grow Rich: Napoleaon Hill

    This book focuses on the power of thinking and belief. It helped me understand how mindset plays a key role in achieving success.

  5. Ikigai: Hector Gracia & Francesc

    A book that explains the concept of finding purpose in life. It give me a new perspective on living a meaningful and happy life.

    

Why I Love Reading

Reading helps me improve my thinking, communication, and focus. It allows me to learn from successful people and apply those leassons in my own life. Books play an important role in shaping my personality and guiding me towards my goals.
